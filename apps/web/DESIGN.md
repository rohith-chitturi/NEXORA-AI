# NEXORA AI Design System

## Overview
NEXORA AI is a next-generation AI-powered autonomous commerce platform. The design must feel handcrafted, premium, and look like a YC-funded AI startup. Inspirations include Apple, Linear, Stripe, Arc Browser, Notion, Raycast, Perplexity, Vercel, and Framer.

## Core Aesthetic Principles
- **No Templates**: Everything must feel bespoke.
- **Whitespace**: Generous, mathematical spacing to allow content to breathe.
- **Glassmorphism**: Soft, blurred backgrounds with subtle translucency for depth.
- **Shadows**: Premium, multi-layered, smooth drop-shadows (not hard or generic).
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, SF Pro, or custom Google Font like Outfit/Space Grotesk) with high contrast and perfect tracking.
- **Borders**: Subtle, semi-transparent borders. Gradient borders for active or premium AI elements.
- **Shapes**: Rounded cards and soft edges (e.g., `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl` for main panels).

## Component Styling
- **Floating Panels**: Sidebars and chat windows should float above the canvas.
- **Buttons**: Sleek, minimal buttons. Primary buttons should have a subtle gradient or glowing effect (AI-feel).
- **Cards**: Smooth hover effects (lift + glow).
- **Command Palette**: A central, beautifully styled quick-action menu.

## Colors
- **Background**: Deep, rich dark mode (e.g., very dark gray/blue, `#09090b` or `#0a0a0a`) or crisp pure white/off-white for light mode.
- **Accents**: Neon or elegant gradients (Purple/Blue/Pink) representing the "AI" intelligence layer.
- **Text**: High contrast for readability, soft grays for secondary information.

## Interactions & Animations
- **Smooth Transitions**: All state changes must have smooth, eased transitions.
- **Micro-animations**: Delightful feedback on clicks, hovers, and focus states.
- **Loading States**: Beautiful skeleton loaders that pulse softly, never jarring spinners unless necessary.

## Example Use Cases
- The AI Shopping Agent chat interface must look native, like a premium IDE or Raycast prompt.
- Product Comparison Tables should feel incredibly clean and easy to scan.
- Deal Finder notifications should pop up as sleek, non-intrusive floating toasts.
